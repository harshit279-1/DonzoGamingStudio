ABOUT DGS

🔥 Our Story

Donzo Gaming Studio was born from a simple belief — that every gamer in Pune deserves a stage to prove themselves. We built the region's most competitive and community-driven esports platform from the ground up.

From casual gaming sessions to intense tournament nights, we create experiences that bring the community together and push players to their absolute limits.
